Short description

Recto: full-length depiction of St George with a sword and a lance, wearing flamboyant armour and facing left against a black background. The defeated dragon crouches in front of the saint. Verso: full-length depiction of St Lawrence, facing left against the backdrop of a landscape. The saint holds a grid as a symbol of his martyrdom as well as an open book that he shows to the viewer. [Görres, cda 2015]
