Short description

The painting shows Christ in an aureole at the apex of the composition, flanked by the Virgin and St John the Baptist, other saints are visible in the bordering band of cloud together with angels' heads. At the left in the foreground St Peter and the archangel lead the naked saved souls into heaven, while at the right Hell is shown as a hole in the ground into which the devil and his demons drive the damned.[Görres, cda 2016]
